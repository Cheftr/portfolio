About Me

Learn more about who I am, my background, and my interests.

   My name is Tim Rishell, a Michigan native with a passion for nature, adventure, culinary arts, and technology. My journey began in the beautiful state of Michigan, where I was born and raised. Since high school, I've been lucky to share my life with my amazing partner, Ariel. We met in 2015 and she is the best thing to ever happen to me, I truly am lucky to share this journey with her.
   My love for learning led me to explore the world of programming in 2017. I've been on a self-driven journey ever since, diving into various programming languages and constantly expanding my knowledge.
   When I'm not coding or working, you'll often find me out in nature. I love hiking, rock climbing, and exploring the great outdoors. I also enjoy unwinding with a good PC video game now and then.
   Thanks for stopping by to learn a bit about me. Feel free to reach out if you share any of these interests or just want to chat!
